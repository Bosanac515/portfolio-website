About Me

💡 Turning Ideas Into Impact

I'm a student at Waukee Northwest High School with a passion for technology, business, and development. As the founder of BosaTek, I help local businesses establish a strong digital presence — building fast, secure websites and configuring the full stack of tools that get small businesses found online.

On the technical side, I specialize in cybersecurity, web development, and business automation. I've built everything from cybersecurity scanners and browser-based games to full client websites — always focused on creating real-world impact, not just completing assignments.

I'm actively growing my skills through hands-on projects, certifications, and real client work — driven by the belief that technology, when applied thoughtfully, can transform any business.
